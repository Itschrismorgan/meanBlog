About Morgans on the Equator

Bienvenidos! Welcome!

I assume, if you are reading this blog, that you must be family, friend or acquaintance of some sort. (I guess you could just be extraordinarily lost on the world wide web.) We are a family of four from Central Kentucky and we have decided to pull up stakes and go on an adventure. We are going to Ecuador for at least six months, likely longer. We hope to post lots of pictures and stories about our travels. It’s our way of keeping all of you in the loop about where we are and how we are doing. Maybe we can convince a few of you to come for a visit (or stay awhile!).
